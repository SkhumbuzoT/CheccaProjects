Our Story

Founded in Johannesburg, CHECCA Trading & Projects has been providing top-quality home improvement solutions to South African homeowners for over a decade. We take pride in our craftsmanship and commitment to excellence.

We believe that your home should be both beautiful and functional, designed to fit your unique lifestyle.

Our team of certified craftsmen and installers bring years of experience to every project, ensuring results that exceed expectations. We use only premium materials and the latest techniques to deliver lasting value.
