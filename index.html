Our Story

CHECCA Home Interiors & Maintenance is a proudly local business offering expert home improvement services. From custom-built cabinetry to countertop installations and general maintenance, we are committed to delivering high-quality work that fits your space, budget, and style.

We believe every home deserves attention to detail — and that's exactly what we provide.

Founded in Johannesburg, our team of skilled craftsmen brings decades of combined experience to every project. We take pride in our South African heritage and approach each job with the care and dedication it deserves.

At CHECCA, our mission is to transform houses into homes through exceptional craftsmanship and reliable service. We're committed to:

  • Delivering quality workmanship that stands the test of time
  • Providing honest, transparent pricing with no hidden costs
  • Building lasting relationships with our clients based on trust
  • Supporting local suppliers and craftsmen

We measure our success by the satisfaction of our clients and the quality of our work, not just the bottom line.
